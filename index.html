Welcome to PIMPRI CHINCHWAD EDUCATION TRUST's Hostel Facility!

At PCET, we understand the importance of providing a comfortable and conducive living environment for our students. That's why we offer exceptional hostel facilities that cater to the needs of our diverse student community. Our aim is to create a home away from home, ensuring that students can focus on their studies while enjoying a vibrant and enriching campus life.
